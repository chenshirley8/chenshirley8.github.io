OUR MISSION
We are a student-run nonprofit that invests in the community and leadership of youth from underserved communities in Cambodia by providing a positive outlet through the sport of Ultimate Frisbee.
Ultimate has been a growing sport in Cambodia.
Apart from costing next to nothing, it can be played in open public spaces, making easily accessible to everyone. With both male and female participants coming from all across the social spectrum, it is unique in its social inclusivity.
